The Art of Fragrance

Understanding Scent Notes

01

Top Notes

The first impression — light, fresh, and fleeting. These are the scents you smell immediately upon application. They typically last 15–30 minutes and include citrus, light fruits, and herbs like bergamot, lemon, and lavender.

BergamotLemonLavenderGrapefruit
02

Heart Notes

The soul of the fragrance — warm, rounded, and full-bodied. These emerge as the top notes fade, lasting 2–4 hours. They form the core identity of the perfume, often featuring florals and spices like rose, jasmine, and cardamom.

RoseJasmineCardamomYlang-Ylang
03

Base Notes

The foundation — deep, rich, and long-lasting. These are the scents that linger on your skin for hours after application. They anchor the entire composition and include woods, resins, and musks like sandalwood, vanilla, and amber.

SandalwoodVanillaAmberMusk
